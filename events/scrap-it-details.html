EVENT DETAILS

SCRAP-IT

Prizes Worth Rs 6,000

Participation type : Individual

Eligibility : Open to all 11th, 12th, Diploma and UG/PG students


An innovation-based competition. The exercise will enable the participants go through major as well as minor issues caused by trash and enhance their thinking skills and creativity.

Objective

1.To present the final products which should be made from waste materials of any kind.
2.The presentation can be based on your understanding, inspiration, ideas and creativity of any field/topic.
3.Free and unconventional thinking is promoted and great ideas are encouraged.

General Rules:

1. Each participant must have to carry their respective college ID.
2. Construction/Assembly of miniature statuettes from scrap materials (for example: Wires, gears, metal sheets, spanners, bolts, screws, nuts, hard plastic etc).
3. Minimum size of statuette - None
4. Exoskeletons can be allowed to be brought from homes.
5.No materials will be supplied except power supply and wifi(Teams will have to bring batteries, laptops on their own if required).
