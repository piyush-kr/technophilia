EVENT DETAILS

ROBO-OLYMPICS

Prizes Worth Rs 24,000

Participation type : Team

Eligibility : Open to all +1, +2 and UG/PG students


EVENT 1 : Robo Sumo Wrestling

Each team competes inside a sumo ring with a robot they constructed themselves around the given specifications. The match starts at the judges command and continues until till either of the contestant's robot touches the ring of the platform.

Specifications

1.Sumo Ring Diameter- 160cm
2.Robot Dimensions Height-unspecified Width-25cm Length-25cm Weight-(approx)1000g
3.The Robot should be controllable through a wireless controller.
Modifications are allowed but should meet the above specifications.

Match Rules:

1.Matches would be done on knockout basis.
2.One match will consist of 3 rounds of 2 minutes.
3.Winner of each round will get a maximum of 1 point each.
4.Winner would be decided on the maximum points earned during the match.
5.Points would be awarded when:
-> (a) A team legally forces the body of the opposing robot to touch the outside of the ring
-> (b) The opposing robot touches the outer ring on its own
-> (c) Opposition teams robot is permanently disarmed during the match.
6.Players can be disqualified if:
-> (a) Derogatory behaviour from the player during the match is found.
-> (b) Obstruction during the match.
-> (c) Cheating of any sort.
7.In case of technical difficulty before a match, players will get a maximum of 3 mins to sort the problem.
8.Any robot not meeting the required specifications would be disqualified.
9.Judges decision will be considered final.

EVENT 2 : Robo Football

A 1v1 football match in which robots of each team would compete against each other.

Specifications

1.Field Length-3.5m Breadth-2.5m
2.Goal Post Width-60cm
3.Robot Dimensions Height-unspecified Width-25cm Length-25cm Weight-(approx)1000g.
4.The Robot should be controllable through a wireless controller.

Match Rules:

1.Matches would be of knockout basis.
2.One match would consist of 2 halves, 3 mins each.
3.Winner would be selected on the basis of maximum goals scored by a player in the match.
4.In case of a draw, winner will be selected on the basis of a penalty shoot out where each player will get 3 tries. Player with maximum goals scored wins.
5.In case of a technical problem, Players can opt for a timeout of 2 mins once in a game. If the problem is not sorted in the allotted time, then the opposing team wins the match.
6.Players can be disqualified if:
-> (a) Derogatory behaviour from the player during the match is found.
-> (b) Obstruction during the match.
-> (c) Cheating of any sort.
-> (d) Robot not meeting the required specification.
7.Referees decision will be final.
