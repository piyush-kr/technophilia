EVENT DETAILS

BRAINIAC

Prizes Worth Rs 7,000

Participation type : Individual

Eligibility : Open to all 11th, 12th, Diploma and UG/PG students


Test your brain thinking skills, inner creativity & general knowledge with some highly engaging mind games!

The event will comprise of 5 rounds with each round having different type of questions that will test your memory, attention span, logic, general knowledge and quick thinking.

Rounds

1. Riddles - The challenge will have a phrase, question or statement having double or veiled meaning put forth as a puzzle to solve.
2. Brain teaser - A puzzle involving lateral thinking forcing us to think critically and outside the box.
3. Logic puzzle - Solving problems using a series of clues involving critical thinking and reasoning skills.
4. Visual brain teaser - A picture will be presented with some patterns or hidden objects. We have to find the answer using creativity and abstract thinking.
5. General knowledge - A set of questions regarding current affairs, science etc.

General Rules and Regulations:

1. Each participant must have to carry their respective college ID.
2. Each round will have 10 questions.
3. Correct answer will net you 2 points. Incorrect answer will deduct 1 point. Blank answer will not affect anything.
4. Winners will be decided on the total point count at the end of all rounds.
5. No mobile phones, smart watches or such electronic devices will be allowed. Anyone found using such devices or other unfair means will be disqualifies.
6. Participants must carry their own pen/pencil.
