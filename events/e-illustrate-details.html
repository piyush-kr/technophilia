EVENT DETAILS

E-ILLUSTRATE

Prizes Worth Rs 12,000

Participation type : Individual

Eligibility : Open to all +1, +2 and UG/PG students


Get ready to flaunt your amazing design skills and build a cool website.
E-Illustrate is an Design and Development event, which will tests your skills for designing and developing a website.
This event will consist of two sub-events DESIGN-Ex and CSSscape.

EVENT 1 : DesignEx

⮚1st round will be the Logo Design round, where the participants will have to design a simple logo based on the client's brief which will be provided on the spot.
⮚Applications Allowed : Adobe Photoshop / Illustrator
⮚Round 1 Duration : 1 hour
⮚2nd round is the UI design round where the participants will have to design a website UI for the same brief.
⮚Applications Allowed : Adobe Photoshop / Figma / Illustrator
⮚Round 2 Duration : 1 hour 30 mins

EVENT 2 : CSSscape

⮚For the 1st round participants will be provided with an HTML file. They will have to use CSS to style the website without altering any content in the HTML file.
⮚Applications Allowed : Any IDE
⮚Round 1 Duration : 1 hour
⮚For the 2nd round, participants will have to develop a responsive landing page based on the Design / Brief provided on the spot.
⮚Applications Allowed : Any IDE
⮚Round 2 Duration : 2 hours

Event Rules and Regulations:

1. Each participant must have to carry their respective college ID.
2. The participants must carry pen/pencil.
3. Rough sheets will be provided by the University.
4. All designs must be original and should not violate any Indian Copyright Laws or Intellectual Property rights of any third party
5. Use of any pre-built templates and CSS libraries will lead to disqualification
6. Use of any other applications other than the ones mentioned above will lead to disqualification.
7. Participants are required to bring their own laptops to participate however computers will be provided to those without devices. (Subject to availability)
8. Participants will have to submit the final file (Logo / UI) along with the Project files and all the images used.
