EVENT DETAILS

INNOVANZA

Prizes Worth Rs 13,000

Participation type : Individual / Group (max 3)

Eligibility : Open to all 11th, 12th, Diploma and UG/PG students


In this one-day Innovation event termed as INNOVANZA, you'll be an active part of a design thinking process. Through individual and small group activities, you'll experience a step-by-step process for identifying and acting on new innovation opportunities and thinking in new ways to capture that potential.
This event will be highly collaborative and interactive. As part of a community of innovation learners and leaders we will engage in a culture of innovation together. This event focuses on the group or team element in organizations, and how you can lead and facilitate them through the innovation process.

As the saying goes "creativity is thinking new things, but innovation is doing new things". So INNOVANZA focuses on transforming the way people think.

General Rules:

Theme : Eco-Friendly Innovation

1. Each participant must have to carry their respective college ID.
2. Open for all students of schools(11th and 12th) and colleges.
3. The individuals are open to bring any ideas, prepared on slides and present them before the judges. (Note:- Working or Demo models are also welcomed but not mandatory)
4. A time-limit of 10-15 minutes will be provided to every presenter.
5. Participants must bring their own laptops.
