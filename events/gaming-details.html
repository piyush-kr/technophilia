EVENT DETAILS

ARCADIA

Prizes Worth Rs 36,000

Participation type : Team

Eligibility : Open to all 11th, 12th, Diploma and UG/PG students


The whole event is divided into two games Valorant and BGMI, students from different universities will be competing for the prize pool. These games will run for two consecutive days. It will be a mixture of online and offline games. The games will be streamed online for everyone to enjoy.

EVENT 1 : Valorant

1.All the games will be played online.
2. 16 Teams will compete in a "best of one" map single elimination bracket.
3. There will be an elimination round before the final 16 teams get qualified (SUBJECT TO REGISTRATIONS).
4. The games will completely be in the format of a knockout tournament, where each team will fight the respective opposite team assigned, where the winner will directly qualify in the later stages, eventually qualifying for the grand finals.
5. The Finals will be contested between the last two teams coming all the way through and qualifying for the Grand Finals, and will compete in a "best of three" maps - match.
6. There will be a fair toss between the IGL's of the respective teams for choosing any map of their preference, and to choose whether they want to attack or defend first.
7. The matches will be played on the latest patch version.

Player Rules and Regulations (VALORANT):

1. No third party application is allowed to alter any kind of performance boost, or any kinds of MODs. If any one of the players is found to be using the same, the team shall get immediately disqualified.
2. The teams participating may not add or drop players from their rosters once they have registered their names.
3. In the event of an emergency at any point during a Match, a team will be given upto ten minutes to produce an eligible substitute on-site. If a replacement cannot be found, the team will forfeit (IT IS SUGGESTED TO REGISTER FOR A SUBSTITUTE PLAYER AT THE TIME OF REGISTRATIONS TO AVOID SUCH DISCREPANCIES).
4. Teams will have no provision to appoint coaches.
5. The players can only be allowed to use the application "DISCORD" for voice chat purposes, or the in-game overlay for voice chat. Other than these two, no other application will be used for voice chat purposes in-game.
6. The Team, as well as the all chat will be moderated by the appointed moderators, and "trash talking" is not allowed at any point of the game.
7. Players will use their own online accounts and Riot IDs during play of the tournament.
8. Players must use their team tag in front of their name for consistency.
9. Every team will be allowed a maximum of "one" timeout in each half for a duration of 30 seconds, making it "two" in the whole match [IN CASE OF ANY TECHNICAL ISSUES, THE TIMEOUT TIME MIGHT BE INCREASED]



EVENT 2 : BGMI

1. All the games will be played offline.
2. Teams will be divided in groups and will be given two matches/maps to play for the prelims and the teams with the most points scored will be selected and sent to finals.
3. The finals will have three matches/maps.
4. Player with the highest kills all over the tournament will be given a special prize (MVP). (In case of two players with the same kill (draw), the players will be given the option of either a 50-50 division of the prize or a 1v1 TDM best of 3 match).
5. If the lobby password is leaked, the responsible team will get a penalty in points.
6. All the teams should bring their own extension board and chargers for their devices.
7. In case of any connectivity issues from our side, the players can play from their cellular connection (JIO doesn't work but AIRTEL has good connectivity in the venue). 8. Point distribution:

1 kill = 1 point

Player Rules and Regulations (BGMI):

1. Players must register with their actual game ID (ACCOUNT LEVEL 40+).
2. Only the registered player can play the game (IT IS SUGGESTED TO REGISTER FOR A SUBSTITUTE PLAYER AT THE TIME OF REGISTRATIONS TO AVOID SUCH DISCREPANCIES). Addition of new player/player ID will not be considered on the game day.
3. No cheating/hacking allowed. Disqualification of the team if caught.
4. Only phones (I-PAD / EMULATOR / TRIGGERS ARE NOT ALLOWED). Finger sleeves are allowed.
5. Matches will start at their respective time. One of the players in a team must record their gameplay for whatever situation arises (LIKE DEFENDING CHEATING ACCUSATION).
6. The players can only be allowed to use the application "DISCORD" for voice chat purposes, or the in-game voice chat. Other than these two, no other application will be used for voice chat purposes in-game.
