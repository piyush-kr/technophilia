EVENT DETAILS

MOBILE PHOTOGRAPHY

Prizes Worth Rs 7,000

Participation type : Individual

Eligibility : Open to only Diploma and UG/PG students


What good is a thought if it remains in itself? Click, Fail and then Click again.
This event will entice you into improving your photography and editing skills. It will be conducted in two phases Photo Walk and Editing Phase

Event Flowchart

⮚In the 1st phase (Photo Walk) all the participants are to walk around the RGU campus to click the photographs with their mobile devices for a certain period of time

⮚2nd phase (Editing) will be conducted in a room where all the participants will gather after the completion of 1st phase.

General Rules and Regulations:

1. Each participant must have to carry their respective college ID.
2. Images captured only by mobiles will be accepted.
3. Participants using any other camera devices like DSLR, point-and-shoot or mirrorless, etc. will be disqualified.
4. Images must be captured in the college premises.
5. A minimum of 5 images and maximum of 6 images must be submitted in a PDF format.
6. Image correction and colour grading/balancing are permitted.
7. Manipulated pictures will be rejected.
8. Title, mobile device information, post-processing applications used, descriptions/vision of photographs must be accompanied with photographs attached.
9. Photographs must be of highest resolution possible.
10. Photographs should not have any watermarks.
